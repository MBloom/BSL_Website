What we do

Today, our customers are challenged to provide more services, better reliability, and flexible capability—within lower budgets. In the past, IT decision-makers decreased costs by cutting services, shelving projects, and espousing system maintenance. This approach sacrifices quality and is counterproductive to the objective of long-term cost savings. The resulting outdated systems hinder the ability to deploy needed applications and services; pose cyber security risks; and strain resources, people, and processes.

Conversely, our approach tackles these modernization challenges by integrating and replacing legacy systems with compatible new technologies to reduce overall costs.

SAMcloud

A workbench that empowers clients

Our team has designed and implemented a modern systems engineering workbench that allows our customers to view project development in real time. We do all of our work directly in SAMcloud, and our clients receive personal accounts for viewing and commenting. This makes our project development process highly collaborative and 100% transparent.

SAM2

A repeatable and scalable approach to systems engineering

We adapt classical systems engineering methods to form our proprietary Systems Assessment & Methodology (SAM2), a repeatable approach for solving complex problems. From deciding which IT enterprise a small business should use to overhauling the largest systems of government organizations, BluestoneLogic’s SAM2 is guaranteed to provide tangible results.
